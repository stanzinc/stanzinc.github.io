About Us

We are a diverse community that includes private investors, financial professionals, retired people managing their super funds, students, and others ranging from highly experienced to beginners. Our members invest in a broad range of financial products: shares, forex, options, derivatives, cryptocurrencies ... and more. STANZ aims to support its members with education to achieve their goals.

We host live educational events in Auckland, New Zealand on a bi-monthly basis.

We are a member society of the International Federation of Technical Analysts Inc (IFTA) that provides access to certified training qualifications and quality webinars, conferences and publications.

What is technical analysis?

Technical analysis is a discipline employed to evaluate investments and identify trading opportunities. It analyses past market information, such as price, volume, volatility and open interest, to forecast and time trading and investing in financial instruments.
